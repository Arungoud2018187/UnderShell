# UnderShell
Women Safety
 UnderShell ‚Äî Women's Safety Application

"UnderShell"is a mobile application designed to enhance women's safety by providing instant assistance, real-time location tracking , and emergency communication with trusted contacts.  
Built with a focus on reliability and usability, UnderShell ensures that help is always just a gesture away ‚Äî anytime, anywhere.

 Features

- Saviors List 
  Add trusted contacts such as parents, guardians, or friends. The app ensures that in emergencies, your saviors are alerted instantly.

- Emergency Gesture Dialing  
  Pre-register simple screen gestures or movements that automatically trigger emergency calls to each contact on your Saviors List ‚Äî no need to unlock or navigate your phone in high-stress moments.

- Real-Time Location Sharing  
  Instantly share your live location with selected contacts during emergencies, helping them track your movement in real-time.

- Instant SMS Alerts
  Send automated SMS messages containing your live location to all registered contacts at the press of a button or via gesture activation.

- Built-in Safety Tools (Optional)
  Future extensions include features like a **siren** and **flashlight** for enhanced safety and attention during emergencies.

- Google Maps Integration
  The app leverages the Google Maps API for live location tracking and visualization.
